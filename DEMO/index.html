About Iron Man

Iron Man, also known as Tony Stark, is a fictional superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. He was created by writer and editor Stan Lee, developed by scripter Larry Lieber, and designed by artists Don Heck and Jack Kirby. He made his first appearance in Tales of Suspense #39 in 1963.

Tony Stark is a billionaire industrialist and genius inventor who creates a powered suit of armor to save his own life and fight evil as Iron Man.
